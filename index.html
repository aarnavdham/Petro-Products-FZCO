About Us

Based in the United Arab Emirates, Anvay Petro Products is a premier international trading enterprise specializing in the strategic sourcing and distribution of Refined Oil Products including all commercial grades of Paraffin Wax, Tar & Asphalt, Plastic & Nylon Raw Materials.

In a volatile global market, we serve as a reliable bridge between world-class refineries and industrial end-users. We manage complex supply chains to deliver essential raw materials that drive infrastructure, energy, and manufacturing sectors forward.

Vision

To transform the global supply chain for Refined Oil Products, Tar & Asphalt, Plastic& Nylon Raw Materials, setting new benchmarks for quality, efficiency, and sustainability, and establishing Anvay Petro Products as the indispensable partner across continents.

Mission

To be the trusted and premier partner in international trading, consistently delivering quality products and exceptional service to our clients globally.

Why Partner with Anvay Petro Products?

We don't just trade products; we manage risk and secure supply chains. With a strong presence in vital trade hubs, we ensure that every transaction complies with global trade regulations, delivering your cargo on time, every time.
